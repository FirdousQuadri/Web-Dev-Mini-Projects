Lago di Braies.

Lago di Braies, also known as Pragser Wildsee, is a stunning alpine lake situated in the Dolomites of South Tyrol, Italy, renowned for its vibrant turquoise-green waters, dramatic mountain backdrop, and the imposing Croda del Becco peak that reflects on its surface; considered a "pearl of the Dolomites," this lake is a popular destination for hikers, nature lovers, and photographers, offering scenic walks along its shores and boat rentals, while its unique origin lies in a massive landslide that blocked the Rio Braies stream creating a natural dam.
